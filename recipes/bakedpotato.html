Baked Potato

A warm, hearty baked potato that can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Large potato
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Optional toppings (cheese, sour cream, chives)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Wash the potato and prick it several times with a fork.
  3. Rub the potato with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  4. Bake for about 45 minutes until tender.
  5. Cut open and add butter and toppings of your choice.
