Lasagna

Yummy delicious lasagna recipie with lots of instructions and it will be delicious when you make it.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Lasanga
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Place frozen lasanga in the microwave.
  2. Heat up the lasanga in the microwave.
  3. Remove the lasagna from the microwave and put it in a plate.
  4. Salt and Pepper to taste.
  5. Drink water with your lasagna to ensure hydration.
