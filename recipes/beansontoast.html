Beans on Toast

A classic and simple dish thatâ€™s perfect for a quick and tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • Baked beans
  • Bread slices
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Heat up the baked beans in a saucepan or microwave.
  3. Butter the toast.
  4. Pour the heated beans over the toast.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
